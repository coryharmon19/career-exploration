Web Designer

Salary: $72,779

Activities: Cover all aspects of creating websites, meet with clients, assess needs, create and maintain, writing/editing content.

Tools/Tech: Adobe Photoshop, Pictaculous, Balsamiq, Lightshot, Sublime Text.

Work Environment: Most web designers do freelance work at home. They may have to travel to reach clients.

Job Skills: Visual designer, UX (user experience), SEO marketing, social media, design software.

Post Secondary

Web Designers do not necessarily require any post secondary education. They can easily be self-taught or learned through high school or online courses.
